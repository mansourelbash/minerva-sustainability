Sustainability

At Minerva Foods, sustainability is at the core of our business model and part of our DNA. We believe that sustainable practices are essential for the long-term success of our company and the well-being of the communities we serve. Road MAP 2025

In 2020, we launched our sustainability plan, managed by a specialized committee in close collaboration with a wide variety of our stakeholders. Aligned with the UN Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs), our plan sets out a series of ambitious short, medium and long-term targets.

Strategy

Minerva's strategy is based on three pillars: Operational Excellence, Supply Chain Management, and Social Impact. We are committed to continuous improvement in all areas of our business, from farm to fork, ensuring the highest standards of quality, safety, and sustainability.

Leveraging our global presence and local expertise, we strive to deliver value to our customers and stakeholders while minimizing our environmental footprint. Our integrated approach ensures that sustainability is embedded in every aspect of our operations, from sourcing to production to distribution.

"We believe that sustainability is not just a responsibility, but an opportunity to lead the industry towards a more sustainable future. Our commitment to environmental stewardship, social responsibility, and economic viability drives everything we do. We are working with our suppliers, customers, and partners to create a more sustainable food system that benefits everyone - from farmers to consumers. Through innovation, collaboration, and transparency, we are building a business that not only meets the needs of today but also preserves resources for future generations. Sustainability is at the heart of our strategy, and we are committed to making a positive impact on the planet and the communities we serve."

- CEO
